My standard to go bag:

-calorie dense rations 
-portable energy source(solar panel)
-source of fire: tinder, matches, magnesium strike
-mylar blankets
-sawyer water filter for microbes
-emergency hand crank radio 
-first aid kit
-extra gauze sponges
-extra medical tape
-tactical gloves
-hammock and emergency tent(mylar or other)
-emergency rope
-whistle
-compass
-water purification tablets
-gas mask
-gun
-utility knife
-Kukri
